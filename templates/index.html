Are you fed up of wasting your time on movies and shows that are not worth it?

Type in your movie name, set up your netflix and let us decide if you should watch it or not!

How does it work?

It scrapes the data for the movie/show from IMDb, analyzes it and gives you well drafted plots, showing you the whole journey of the movie/show.

It also shows you how many people recommend watching it using the weighted average of the reviews given by IMDb users.
